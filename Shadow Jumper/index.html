Shadow Jumper

Control two characters - one in light, one in shadow.

They move oppositely. When one jumps, the other ducks.

Reach the goal without either character dying!

How to Play

  • Use LEFT/RIGHT arrow keys to move
  • Press UP to make Light character jump (Shadow character will duck)
  • Press DOWN to make Light character duck (Shadow character will jump)
  • Guide both characters to their respective goals
  • If either character falls or hits an obstacle, you lose
